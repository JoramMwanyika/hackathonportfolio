WhistleWire Forensic AI

Problem

Many citizens, especially in underrepresented or repressive regions, witness critical events but lack a safe, anonymous, and trusted way to report them. At the same time, journalists struggle to verify tips quickly due to the rise of misinformation and manipulated media.

Solution

I came up with WhistleWire which is a secure, AI-powered web platform that allows citizens to anonymously submit news tips, while enabling verified journalists to analyze submissions using AI tools for media forensics, sentiment detection, and misinformation verification.

Shamba Assistant

Problem

Smallholder farmers in Kenya lack timely access to personalized agricultural advice, weather forecasts, pest management support, and market pricing. Many also face challenges due to language barriers, low literacy, and limited internet access.

Solution

I came up with Shamba Assistant which is an AI-powered chatbot accessible via WhatsApp and USSD that provides localized crop guidance, weather-based planting advice, pest diagnosis, and real-time market prices in both English and Swahili — empowering farmers to make smarter, timely, and profitable decisions.
